Cat-A-Combs

The world is dying.
Bingus, The Mage of Light, once a bulwark against the evils of the world - has betrayed us all. His fortresses, once bastions of good, had been morphed into labyrinths containing portals to the 7 hells. First local towns fell to the legions that spewed out of them. Life itself was exterminated in their wake, replaced with all manners of abhorrent creations from the now fallen mage Bingus. Soon cities fell to the legions of the hells, their inhabitants turned into mutated servants, a sick mockery of their former selves. The empires in which these cities once stood soon too fell one by one, and as the threat posed by this invasion had become glaringly apparent, even once bitter foes began to put aside their differences to stop the rising tide.
As it became increasingly clear that Bingus` fortresses were the source of the forces of the hells, expeditions composed of the worlds strongest: mages, warriors, and paladins alike were launched within them to halt the flow once and for all. Some were successful, destroying the bridge between the hells and our world, but most were not, only adding fuel to the ever burning fire.
The legions pushed ever forwards, having purged entire continents of life. It is within the last great empire, Nekotaria - once the home of Bingus, that the world makes its last stand. Within its capital's walls lies the seemingly dormant fortress of Mori. Vagrants, legends, criminals, exiles and heroes alike attempt to reach its depths, whether for fame, wealth, or atonement - and your party is no different. But time is running out, and this may be the last chance to save anyone or anything.
